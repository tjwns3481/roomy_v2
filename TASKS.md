- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui

- **AI**: OpenAI GPT-4o
- **Crawler**: Puppeteer
- **Payment**: Toss Payments
- **Maps**: Naver Map, Kakao Map
- **Deploy**: Vercel
